What Does This Site Do?


This site displays the analytics of submissions on a schoology discussion page.
The purpose of this site is to make grading Schoology Discussion Pages easier.

Instructions


1. All the fields must be entered. For the "paste here" box, you must go to the schoology discussion page, do ctrl+A and ctrl+C to copy all the text on the page, and paste it in the box
2. After all the fields are entered correctly, click the "Search" button. This will open a new page with a table of analytics of submissions

Site is not working?


if the site is not working, then, no worries! Make sure you followed this checklist:

- all fields were entered

- date is in format day/month/year. not month/day/year

- time is in proper format: "hour:minute am/pm". example: "8:00 am"

- in the schoology discussion page, make sure all the comments/replies are opened up. if not, click on the "expand tab" symbol under "Write a comment"
